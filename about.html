    Our Journey

    Mr. Mayjohn P J, also known as Mayjohn Findjoy, is the CEO and founder of Event Aura Management Company. Under his leadership, the company has achieved significant milestones in the event management industry...

    Event Aura began as a dream in 2008, when our founder, Mayjohn P J, envisioned a company that would revolutionize the event management industry. Starting from humble beginnings in the vibrant city of Thrissur, Kerala, we embarked on a journey to create unforgettable event experiences that resonate deeply with our clients and their guests.
