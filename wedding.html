    Artful Weddings

    Designing an event is one thing—creating an immersive, unforgettable experience is something else entirely. We happen to believe a love like yours deserves the latter.     Expert Wedding Planning

    With over a decade of experience as Wine Country’s most trusted wedding planners and designers—and over 500 multifaceted events under our belt—we know just how to get you from “I honestly have no idea what I want.” to “This is SO us!” We’re here to help you hone a vision that speaks to your story and style, labor over logistics and manage minutia so you don’t have to, and navigate the nuanced world of vendor management (…and in-laws) with poise, professionalism, and plenty of fun along the way.
